A Transforming History

The Rebellion

In the beginning of the era of Transformers the Quintessons ruled over the Transformers, they were slaves
who did the work of the Quintessons. After eons of servitude the transformers decided
it was time to rebel against their oppressors and thus the Autobots were born. The leader
of the Autobots was given a relic of the planet, from the core itself, called The Matrix of Leadership,
which held a great power within it. When the leader died the Matrix was passed down
to someone who was worthy. As the war continued, the autoboots began to lose against the Quintessons,
until they discovered that they could transform (go from their robot mode to a vehicle mode that they had since they
had been created) and the war started to shift in the Autobots favor once more. Eventually as the war raged on
the Autobots won and the Quintessons fled, thus the Transformes entered into a Golden Age.

The Golden Age

The Golden Age of the Transformers is when their technological prowess was at their highest,
they traveled across the stars searching different planets using a device called a Space Bridge.
The Space Bridge opened up a wormhole to another planet, it basically turned a thousand-mile journey
into a one mile journey. As time passed new transformers began to be built, they were stronger
than their predecessors and they could fly, and thus they began to crave power. The leader
of these new transformers was Megatron, and they called themselves the Decepticons, as they gained more
power a group to repel them appeared called the Autobots, and this was the start of the transformer civil war.

Civil War

The primary reason for the civil war was to gain control over Cybertron, Megatron wanted to rule
the planet and the Autobots wanted freedom for all sentient life. As the war raged
on for many millennia Megatron killed many primes until the burden was finally placed on the shoulders
of Optimus Prime. Megatron was a gladiator who fought in the Pits of Kaon (a renowned gladiatorial arena)
he was an expert fighter who had speed, power, and experience in combat while originally Optimus Prime
(or Orion Pax before he was remodeled) was a librarian in the Hall of Records (the most
important library on the planet, it holds all the transformers history). After several millennia of fighting
the energy that fueled them, Energon, began to run out, and the war started to shift to fighting to
preserve their current supplies of Energon and capturing and the few remaining unclaimed stashes left.

Corruption and Escape

Megatron in his lust for power began to search for Dark Energon, a corruptive substance that
destroyed all who came in contact with it, once he found it, he planned on injecting
it into the core of Cybertron (the core creates Energon that the Tranformers used) in order
to control the planet. After the core was liberated from the control of the Decepticons by
Optimus and his elite fighters, the core had to power down for millions of years to
rid itself of the Dark Energon, and because of this the planet could no longer
sustain life. This is when all the transformers, except the leaders and a small portion
of their armies, began to flee the planet in order to search for a new home amongst
the stars. After a while the leaders, and what was left of their armies,
left Cybertron and after a taxing fight they landed on Earth 65 million years ago.

Earth

The remaining armies (which were basically the 20 or so elites on each side who survived)
were in stasis until the 21st century where a small tremor caused a Decepticon to wake up
and heal the other Decepticons. In a failed attempt kill the autoboots the blasted the cave which
caused one of the autoboots to fall into the reaping area of the ship and thus
the Autobots began to be repaired. Once the Decepticons woke up they began to steal energy
in order to make Energon cubes so they could go back to Cybertron and reclaim
their home world; however, the Autobots stopped them every time. To this day the Autobots and
Decepticons continue fight each other until the other is finally wiped out.
